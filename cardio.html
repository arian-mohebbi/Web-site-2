Cardio Myths vs. Facts

Many people believe in cardio misconceptions—let’s clear them up!

hover inside

Myth:

  • More cardio means faster weight loss.
  • Running is the only effective cardio exercise.
  • You should always do cardio on an empty stomach.
  • Low-intensity cardio is useless.

Fact:

  • Excessive cardio can lead to muscle loss. Balance is key!
  • Cycling, rowing, jump rope, and HIIT are just as effective.
  • While fasted cardio may work for some, eating a small snack boosts performance.
  • Steady-state cardio helps with fat oxidation and endurance!
